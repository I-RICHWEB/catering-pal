Overview

Catering-Pal is a web application that is designed to assist all cooks and food lovers to learn and understanding the ingredients and descriptions of some of the best food in the world. It helps individuals who have an interest in learning how to make a variety of simple meals for themselves, family, or even for small and large events.

Beneficial for:

• Families who love learning, trying out foreign foods at home.

• Chiefs and Caterers who want to dazzle their clients with new and varieties of meals on their occasions.

• Singles and Pringles, or students who want to learn and make simple, rich foods that take less time to make.
